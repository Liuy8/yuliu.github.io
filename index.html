About Me


I am now a postdoctoral researcher under the supervision of Prof. Tinne Tuytelaars, at the Processing Speech and Images (PSI) group of KU Leuven. I obtained the Ph.D. from Media Lab of Leiden University in 2018, under the supervision of Prof. Michael S. Lew. Previously, I got both Bachelor and Master degrees from Dalian University of Technology in 2011 and 2014, respectively. During the master study, I was advised by Prof. He Guo and worked with Dr. Qi Jia and Prof. Xin Fan.
